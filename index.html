Core Initiatives

Our Main Pillars of Town Action

Photo Slot: Local Grants Grassroots Wellness Funding

Support Public Health Efforts & Address Needs

We proactively fund innovative public health program tracks that flatten structural economic gaps, address climate variations, and promote sustainable localized wellness. Small mini-grants are regularly distributed town-wide to launch active ideas.

Recognize Public Health Leaders & Programs

We award the Brookline Public Health Leadership Award (honoring Alan Balsam) and the Herb Carlin Community Health Award to outstanding health forces.

Organize Annual Public Health Forums

Drawing hundreds of local citizens together, our interactive policy forums offer structural education pathways. Past panel spaces addressed healthcare access realities, prescription pricing barriers, and explicit health advocacy configurations for vulnerable LGBTQ+ town youth.
